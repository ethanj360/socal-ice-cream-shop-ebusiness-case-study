# SoCal Ice Cream Shop – eBusiness Case Study

This repository contains a strategic business and eCommerce transformation case study focused on modernizing a legacy retail ice cream shop through digital systems, eBusiness planning, and process improvement.

The project analyzes the company’s competitive position and proposes a full Business to Consumer (B2C) eBusiness strategy to improve customer acquisition, retention, and long term growth.

---

## Project Overview

SoCal Ice Cream Shop is a small, legacy business facing declining sales due to outdated technology, limited digital presence, and increasing competition. This case study evaluates the business using established strategy frameworks and designs a modern eBusiness solution centered on eCommerce, mobile commerce, and digital marketing.

The proposed solution focuses on:
- Improving operational efficiency
- Expanding revenue streams through online ordering and merchandise
- Strengthening customer engagement and loyalty
- Supporting scalable long term growth
